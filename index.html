Army Time

This photo was taken when I was in the army, Maybe around 19 years old. I was tasked with organizing and distributing ammo. I remeber I really wanted to smoke but smoking next to live ammo is not the best idea. This was my 1st real job and one of my life goals. I got to experience a pretty interesting life style and activities from being in nature for days on end, parachuting, learning how to use different firearms, tactics and teamwork. I also learned to move on and try other things as well.
